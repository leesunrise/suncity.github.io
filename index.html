Landscapes & Homes

Interior

This is a picture of the interior of Sunnyville. It is basically styled in wood and white. Furniture structure and color can be changed according to the personality of the residents. If you tell a Sunnyville employee when you sign a move-in contract, an outside company will visit and remodel it before you move in.

Exterior

This is the exterior of Sunnyville. A large lake was created and houses were placed around it to create a house with a lake view. There is a promenade around the lake, so you can take a walk at any time. In addition, the plaza has been made large, and there are also several auxiliary facilities.

Facilities

  • Swimming pool

    We have two swimming pools, 1 outdoor pool and 1 indoor pool. Both pools are large enough to accommodate about 100 people. We strive to maintain cleanliness at all times, and prohibit entry in the evening for safety reasons.

  • Big playground

    A large playground is provided for a variety of outdoor activities. I planted grass for fear of joint injury. This place is also prohibited from entering in the evening for safety reasons, so if there is a special reason, you can apply for permission.

  • Cafeteria

    We have prepared a cafeteria for the convenience of meals. If you are tired of preparing food or tired of delivery food, visit this place where residents gather in groups of two or three. Have fun with your neighbors.

  • Grocery store

    There is a grocery store in Sunnyville. We operate from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm, so if you visit here and purchase the products you need or make a delivery request, we can deliver at a fixed time, so please let us know.

  • Fitness center

    This is an exercise center to train one's physical strength indoors for healthy physical activities. Various recreational lectures are also prepared, so if you are interested, you can apply and take them.

  • Medical room

    We have a medical system that can manage your health condition. We manage the health of residents by introducing the latest equipment. Medical staff are always present, so if you have any problems, you can receive medical attention immediately.
